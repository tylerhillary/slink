1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Slink, operated by Radius Technology, uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our websites, use our mobile experiences, or engage with our mentorship platform (collectively, the “Services”). It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

By continuing to browse or use our Services, you agree that we can store and access essential cookies on your device. You can manage or disable non-essential cookies at any time as outlined below.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer, tablet, or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies store information about your browsing session that can be retrieved later. Related technologies—such as web beacons, pixels, device identifiers, and local storage—perform similar functions and are treated as “cookies” for the purposes of this policy.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use first-party and third-party cookies to support four key objectives:

  • Essential Operations: Maintain secure logins, session continuity, and load balancers to deliver core platform functionality.
  • Performance & Analytics: Understand how learners and mentors use Slink in order to improve features, fix errors, and optimise onboarding flows.
  • Personalisation: Remember your preferences—such as language, region, and saved filters—to streamline recurring visits.
  • Marketing: Deliver relevant updates about new skills, mentors, or promotions and measure the effectiveness of our campaigns.

4. Types of Cookies We Set

Cookie Category Purpose Examples Retention
Essential Authentication, security, load balancing, fraud prevention Session ID, CSRF token Session or up to 12 months
Analytics Measure usage trends, diagnose performance, test new features Google Analytics, Mixpanel 6–24 months
Personalisation Remember preferences, saved mentors, and recently viewed skills User locale, product tour state Up to 12 months

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by trusted third parties who provide services on our behalf. These providers may collect information about your online activities over time and across different services. We contractually require partners to process data in accordance with applicable privacy laws and in line with our instructions.

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Refer to the help section of your preferred browser for instructions.
  • Consent Banner: When you first arrive on Slink, you can accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can revisit these choices through the “Cookie Preferences” link in our footer.
  • Third-Party Opt-Outs: Services such as Google Analytics Opt-out or AboutAds provide additional controls.

Blocking essential cookies may impact your ability to sign in, schedule sessions, or access certain secure areas of the platform.

7. Do Not Track Signals

Our Services currently do not respond to “Do Not Track” signals because there is no uniform technological standard for recognising and responding to them. We will revisit this approach if industry standards evolve.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology, regulations, or our business practices. When we make material changes we will update the “Last Updated” date and, where appropriate, provide additional notice (such as a banner or email communication).
