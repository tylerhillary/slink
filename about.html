Our Story

Slink thrives because people build it together.

Founded by Engr. Hillary Tyler and shaped by the relentless dedication of Christiana Ita and Divine-Angel, Slink grew from a bold idea into a living community where verified mentors and learners rise together.

Founded January 2025
Core Team Radius Technology
Community 180+ professionals

From a simple question to a shared mission

Slink began when Engr. Hillary Tyler asked a deceptively simple question: “What if professionals could trade experience with the same ease they book a video call?” The answer required more than technology. It demanded trust, curation, and the kind of operational excellence that only comes when talented people join forces.

That is why Hillary partnered with two remarkable builders. Christiana Ita transformed our operational backbone, ensuring every mentor is verified and every learner gets concierge-level support. Divine-Angel crafted the product experience that makes every interaction feel personal, guided, and worthwhile.

Together they turned a vision into a platform that feels human by design—an exchange built on credibility, empathy, and measurable outcomes.

“We succeed because we build with people who care as deeply as we do about every connection. Christiana and Divine-Angel are the heartbeat behind Slink’s promise.”
— Engr. Hillary Tyler, Founder & CEO
